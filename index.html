    Happy Birthday,
    My Favourite Chapter

    Every memory with you deserves its own page.
    Here's ours — from the very first day, to right now.

    Chapter 01

    Where It All Began

    I don't remember deciding to notice you — I just did. One glance, and somehow the rest of the room got quieter. I didn't know it yet, but that was the first line of us.

    Chapter 02

    Our First Date

    Nervous laughs, too much conversation for one evening, and coffee that went cold because neither of us wanted to stop talking. I remember thinking — I hope this isn't the only time.

    Chapter 03

    Our First Photo Together

    Neither of us posed for it properly, and it's still my favourite picture on my phone. Proof that even at the very beginning, we already looked like we belonged in the same frame.

    Chapter 04

    Our First Concert

    Loud music, terrible phone signal, and your hand in mine the entire night. I don't remember a single song they played — I just remember singing along with you.

    Chapter 05

    Our First Garba

    Lights strung up like stars above us, and you twirling like you were born for that beat. I spent half the night dancing, and the other half just watching you shine.

    Chapter 06

    Your First Birthday With Me

    I wanted that day to feel like you — bright, a little chaotic, and full of love. Watching you smile at your own cake is still one of my favourite things I've ever seen.

    Chapter 07

    Our First Diwali

    Diyas in every corner, your favourite song playing somewhere in the background, and you laughing at something silly I said. That's the kind of light I want in every year with you.
